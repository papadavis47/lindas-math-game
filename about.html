Linda's Math Game

My inspiration for this game was my daughter, Linda Estrella Davis, 7 years old, who teaches me something everyday.

She also provided voice over and served as my consultant - all for the bargain price of a $26 "smart watch" she found on Amazon.com.

Her 10 year old brothers, Diego & Santiago, provided unpaid help with the color choices. Even though they won't be happy with the "unpaid" part.
