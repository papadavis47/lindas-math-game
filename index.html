Linda's Math Game

Welcome to my math game!

You can choose to do addition, subtraction, multiplication or division. For every answer you get right, you will gain 5 points.

For every incorrect answer you will lose 5 points - but you can choose to see the answer or you can choose to try again for more points.

Which ever you choose is okay. You will learn something!

Click anywhere outside this box to get started!
