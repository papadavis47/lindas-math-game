Linda's Math Game

Welcome to our math game!

You can choose to do addition, subtraction, multiplication or division. For every answer you get right, you will gain 5 points. For every incorrect answer you will lose 5 points, but you can choose to see the answer or you can choose to try again for more points. Whichever you choose is okay. You will learn something!

Click below to get started and you will see your options!
